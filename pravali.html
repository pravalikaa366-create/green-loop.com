About Us

GreenLoop is a student-driven venture that enables easy e-waste collection and recycling inside campuses. Students can schedule pickups or drop old chargers, headphones, laptops, and electronic waste through a simple app/portal. Collected items are sent weekly to authorized recyclers, promoting eco-friendly practices and reducing toxic waste.

How It Works

  • Register or log in to your GreenLoop account.
  • Schedule a pickup or choose a nearby drop zone.
  • Our volunteers collect and send e-waste to recyclers weekly.
